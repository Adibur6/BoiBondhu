About Us

OUR MISSION

They says, "A book is not only a friend, it makes friends for you. When you have possessed a book with mind and spirit, you are enriched. But when you pass it on you are enriched threefold."

Our mission is to spread the love of books to a wide community and make people engaged in this process musch as possible.

OUR STORY

As fellow Under-graduate students at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology in 2022, Ashfaq, Zerin & Tahlil Abrar noticed a shift in the way of people's admiration for books. They noticed that people are no longer interested in reading real books â€” instead, they are fond of reading PDF more often, which is also leading them to severe eye problems or chronic headaches. In excuse, they may point out the issue of buying cost or the hassle to buy and store books frequently.

From this shift, an idea was born: BoiBondhu.

Boi-bondhu is a place where one can give-away their old books to free up space for new ones.Thus they can save the buying cost. It's also hassle free, as the whole process is online.

OUR IMPACT

When a book travels from person to person, it not only enhances its reader's mind, but also leaves a good impact on nature. Paper production needs wood, and by re-using one book more than once, we are lessening deforestation at a certain level. Thus it's a green system.

By exchanging books people are making a community of readers. This is helping them to build a quality full social relation.
