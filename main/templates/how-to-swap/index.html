How to Swap

Step

1

Type In

Go to your profile, click the button “Add books” and then type in the details of your book. As soon as we receive all the information about your book, it will be available in the browsing page, and other users can send requests for this book.

Step

2

Receive Request

Check the notification bell if you've received any book request. Once you get a book request, you can either decline or approve that request. If you approve the user request, after a successful delivery, you will receive an estimated amount of coins.

Ask For Books

Scroll through the browsing page, or search by name of specific book. If you find any book interesting, and you have sufficient coins to purchase it, send a request. You can cancel your request, as long as the owner won’t accept your request. Once the owner accepts your request, you will find the book in your waiting list.

Step

3

Step

4

Mail Your Books

After confirming the book request, the sender will mail/ deliver the book towards the receiver. While the book is in this delivery process it will show up in the pending list for the sender and waiting list for the receiver. Once the receiver gets the book and he confirms it by pressing successfully, the sender gets his reward (specified coins) and the process ends here.
